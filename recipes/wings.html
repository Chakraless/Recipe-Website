Wings

Wings are a popular dish known for their flavorful and crispy texture. Typically made from chicken wings, they are seasoned, deep-fried, and then coated in various sauces, such as buffalo, barbecue, or teriyaki. They are often served with celery sticks and ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping. Wings are a favorite appetizer or snack, enjoyed at parties, sports events, or as a main course at restaurants. Their versatility and delicious taste make them a beloved choice for many occasions.

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Seasonings
  • Flour or batter
  • Oil for frying
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Teriyaki sauce
  • Celery sticks
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing
